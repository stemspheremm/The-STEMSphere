Background

STEMSphere was founded on July 29, 2024, by a group of high school students with a shared passion for making STEM education accessible to everyone. As a completely non-profit organization, STEMSphere is dedicated to empowering individuals of all backgrounds, including people with disabilities and marginalized communities. With a strong focus on improving non-formal STEM education in Myanmar, STEMSphere strives to inspire curiosity, innovation, and lifelong learning in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Vision

To inspire and empower students to explore STEM with passion and innovation.

Mission

To deliver innovative STEM learning experiences that enhance critical thinking, problem solving, and creativity.
