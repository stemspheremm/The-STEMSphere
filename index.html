Frequently Asked Questions

STEMSphere is a youth-led, non-profit organization in Myanmar dedicated to empowering students through innovative, inclusive, and accessible STEM education, leadership opportunities, and creative engagement.

Anyone with a passion for science, technology, engineering, or mathematics is welcome to participate—regardless of age, gender, or location. We are committed to inclusivity and diversity.

STEMSphere follows a transparent and structured governance model. Our direction is overseen by a Board of Trustees, with execution led by an Executive Committee and specialized subcommittees to ensure effective decision-making and accountability.

Membership applications are opened periodically on our website and social media platforms. Roles for interns, assistants, and officers are open for application, while managerial and directorial positions are typically offered based on leadership and contribution, not through application.
