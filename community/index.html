Education Partnership

SLC Advocates - Advocates for Sustainable Lifestyle and Culture is a youth-led local environmental organization founded on October 1, 2020. We focus on raising awareness about sustainability and environmental protection through education and active engagement on social media, especially Facebook and LinkedIn. As a media partner, we collaborate with STEMSphere by sharing campaigns, amplifying events, and promoting digital and environmental awareness together. We warmly welcome future collaboration. Reach out to us via slcadvocates2020@gmail.com or public.relations.teamslc@gmail.com, and follow us on Facebook.
